❓ Frequently Asked Questions

How will I receive my purchase?

After successful payment, an email with your download link will be sent automatically to the email address you provided at checkout. Please check your spam/promotions folder if you don't see it.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept GCash, Maya, Credit/Debit Card, and QR Ph — all powered by PayMongo.

Can I resell these products?

Products with Master Resell Rights (MRR) or PLR can be resold. Please check the product description for specific licensing terms.

Do you offer refunds?

Due to the digital nature of our products, all sales are final. However, if you experience technical issues with your download, please contact us and we'll make it right.

Do I need technical skills to use these products?

No! All our products are designed for non-techies. They come with instructions and are ready to use out of the box.

How do I use a coupon code?

Add your items to cart, then enter your coupon code in the "Have a coupon?" field before checkout. The discount will be applied automatically.

I have more questions. How can I reach you?

Click the Contact tab in the navigation menu or message us on Facebook Messenger. We respond within 24 hours!
